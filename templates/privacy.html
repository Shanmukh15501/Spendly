Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps, used solely for security purposes.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly:

  • Displaying your expenses and summaries within the app.
  • Authenticating your account and keeping it secure.
  • Generating category breakdowns and spending reports for you.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database accessible only to authorised systems running Spendly. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to function:

  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log the request; no personal data from your Spendly account is shared.

We do not integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media trackers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request data deletion, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
